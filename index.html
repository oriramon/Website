About

Hi, my name is Ori. I'm a 29-year-old American-Israeli photographer and student, residing in Tel Aviv. Born in Israel and raised in Boston, my passion for photography was born when I dusted off my dad's old Canon AE1 film camera at 15 - one of my first captures being a long exposure of the Golden Gate Bridge. My photography style seeks to capture the artisanry of human movement.
